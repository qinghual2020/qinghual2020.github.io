Qinghua Liu

 

I am a postdoctoral researcher at Microsoft Research in New York City. I study machine learning for sequential decision-making.

I received my Ph.D. from Princeton, advised by Chi Jin. My PhD research focused on partially observable RL (POMDP, PSR, Survey), multi-agent RL (Stochastic Game), and RL with large state spaces (Function Approximation).

During summer 2022, I interned at DeepMind, working with Csaba Szepesvári and Gellért Weisz. Previously, I received a B.E. degree in Electrical Engineering and a B.S. degree in Mathematics from Tsinghua University.
